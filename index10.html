About

In a small back alley in Tokyo, there is a café which has been serving carefully brewed coffee for more than one hundred years. But this coffee shop offers its customers a unique experience: the chance to travel back in time. It tells of a café in Tokyo that allows its customers to travel back in time, as long as they return before their coffee gets cold.

Author- Toshikazu Kawaguchi
